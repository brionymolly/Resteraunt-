Lets get to know each other....

What are we?

Established in 2017, we are not your usual cup of tea. We like to try experimental tastes and combinations to give you an experience you will not forget in a hurry! We also provide the classics such as Darjeeling, Ceylon, Assam and of course Earl Grey. Our coffee side is our party side - we like to give you alatte for your money and give you the energy boost you need for your walk to work or that essay that is due in by tomorrow.

Communiteas

We like to give back. Bit by bit, the donations we raise by your custom go out to the hardworkers who make out tea and coffee sales possible. Currently out project is to provide education for the tea workers in the Assam province, as everyone deserves the right to a good education and a great cup of tea.
